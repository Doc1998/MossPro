About MossPro

The Local Team Behind Kildare's Cleanest Roofs

We're a small, dedicated team of exterior cleaning specialists based right here in County Kildare. We started MossPro because we believed homeowners deserved better — better results, fairer prices, and people they could actually trust on their roof.

Our Story

How MossPro Started

MossPro started the way most good ideas do — from seeing a problem that needed solving. Too many homeowners across Kildare were paying over the odds for roof cleaning that was either rushed, damaging, or both. We knew there was a better way.

We set up MossPro with a simple promise: do quality work, charge a fair price, and treat every home like it's our own. That approach has taken us cleaning roofs across County Kildare — from Maynooth to Newbridge, Celbridge to Kilcock.

Today, we're proud to be one of Kildare's most trusted exterior cleaning companies.

About Us

Kildare’s Roof Cleaning Crew

Our specialist soft wash roof cleaning safely removes moss, algae, and lichen without damaging your tiles. We treat every roof with a biocide to prevent regrowth, keeping your home looking its best for years to come.

Benefits of Regular Roof Cleaning:

  • Prevents moss and algae from freezing, expanding and causing damage
  • Enhances your home’s curb appeal and value
  • Ensures gutters remain free of debris falling from the roof
