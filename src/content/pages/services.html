Roof Cleaning Kildare

Moss, algae, and lichen do more than make your roof look tired — they hold moisture against your tiles, loosen pointing, and can block gutters. Our professional soft wash roof cleaning system removes all biological growth safely, without the high pressure that damages tiles. Every clean includes a biocide treatment to keep your roof moss-free for longer.

Gutters

Gutter Cleaning Kildare

Blocked gutters are one of the most common — and most overlooked — causes of damp in Irish homes. Leaves, moss, and debris build up fast, causing overflows that stain walls, damage foundations, and create ideal conditions for mould. MossPro clears and flushes your full gutter system so water goes where it should — away from your home.
